# UX Principles

## Product Bar

RecipAI must be good enough that a non-technical family member chooses it while cooking. Polish is part of the requirement.

## Mobile First

- Design phone screens first.
- Use large tap targets and predictable bottom navigation.
- Keep text readable at kitchen distance.
- Avoid dense forms on the first screen.
- Make empty, loading, and error states calm and actionable.

## Ask First

The home screen is the Ask experience. It should invite natural-language recipe prompting and saved-recipe modifications without looking like a chatbot.

## Structured Output

AI results render as recipe cards, ingredient lists, step sections, summaries, and actions. Do not default to chat bubbles or raw markdown.

## Visual Direction

- Light surfaces.
- Deep green primary accent.
- Tomato/coral action accent.
- Slate text.
- Subtle borders.
- Radius of 8px or less for most cards and controls.
- No marketing landing page.
- No decorative gradient orbs.
