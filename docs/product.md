# Product

RecipAI is a private family cooking app that runs locally and is used from phones or tablets on the home network.

## Goal

Make it easy to prompt AI for dinner ideas, modify saved recipes, save useful results, plan dinners, generate shopping lists, and cook from a clean mobile interface.

## Audience

The primary audience is a busy family cook who will reject clunky software. The product must feel fast, obvious, polished, and useful in the kitchen.

## V1 Scope

- Ask AI for a new structured recipe.
- Pick a saved recipe and ask for changes.
- Save, rate, find, and cook recipes.
- Randomly assign saved dinners over a date range.
- Generate a shopping list from planned dinners, defaulting to 14 days.
- Run locally with SQLite.

## Non-Goals

- Cloud sync.
- Multi-household accounts.
- Public recipe sharing.
- Generic chatbot experience.
- Native mobile app distribution.
